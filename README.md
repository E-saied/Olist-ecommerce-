# Olist E-commerce Business Analysis

## Project Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive Business Intelligence and Data Analytics project based on the **Olist Brazilian E-commerce Public Dataset**.

The project answers multiple business questions covering customer behavior, seller performance, revenue growth, logistics operations, payment patterns, and product intelligence. Each business question is analyzed using Python and supported by business insights, visualizations, reports, and presentations.

---

## Business Questions

- ✅ Business Question 1 – Delivery & Logistics Operations (SLA Analysis)
- ✅ Business Question 2 – Revenue & Business Growth
- 🚧 Business Question 3 – Customer Behavior & Retention
- 🚧 Business Question 4 – Seller Performance & Distribution
- 🚧 Business Question 5 – Payments & Financial Patterns
- 🚧 Business Question 6 – Product & Category Intelligence
