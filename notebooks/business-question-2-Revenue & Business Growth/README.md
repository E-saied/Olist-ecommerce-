# Business Question 2 – Revenue & Business Growth

## Objective
Analyze Olist's revenue performance and business growth between 2016 and 2018.

## Analysis Includes

- Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Monthly Order Volume
- Top Revenue Categories
- Freight Cost vs Order Value

## Key Findings

- GMV = 13.59M BRL
- AOV = 137.75 BRL
- Revenue peaked during Black Friday 2017.
- Health & Beauty generated the highest revenue.
- Freight cost has a moderate positive correlation with order value (r = 0.41).

## Tools

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
